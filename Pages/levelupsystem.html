Leveling Up │ Earn Roles and Boost Your Experience

Leveling roles are all about celebrating your engagement and achievements. The more you interact and participate, the higher you climb in our community ranks. Each role signifies your dedication and it’s our way of saying thanks for being an active part of our community!

How It Works

➤ Chat & Connect — Every message you send helps you gain experience points (XP). More activity means more XP!

➤ Roles — As you accumulate XP, you'll unlock new roles that showcase your progress and contributions.

➤ Perks — As you progress through the levels, you’ll unlock exclusive secret commands and perks unique to each role.
